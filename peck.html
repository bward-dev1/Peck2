🌽The Great Crispening

In the depths of Stanley Park, beneath roots older than the city itself, lies the Mycelium Core. When Bert pecked it, reality folded like warm bread. He became many things at once: chicken, toaster, knight, toilet seat, and Vancouver’s first 5D entity.

This moment is known as The Great Crispening, the dawn of the Bertwardian Era where code and corn became one.
